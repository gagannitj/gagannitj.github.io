About Us
Our partners
Sun Trail Travels was started by a local villager turned entrepreneur, Tsering Dorjay. Dorjey is from a remote village in Sumda Chenmo and started Sun Trail Travels as a trekking and homestay company in Ladakh to promote income generation in the remote villages of Ladakh. We provide experience based customized Itineraries for trekkers and mountain enthusiasts. Sun Trails through its initiative Green Himalayan Store also promotes Local village handicrafts and organic products.
